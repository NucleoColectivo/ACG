Who We Are. Clarity Through Construction

GIRALDO AEC Solutions is a New York-based company specializing in the coordination, fabrication, and integration of architectural systems across commercial, residential, and institutional environments.

We support design teams, developers, and builders with a strong foundation in technical execution, materials knowledge, and a detail-oriented mindset. We operate at the intersection of design intent and buildability.

“We believe in clarity through construction. Each project deserves detail, discipline, and a tailored response.”

— Philosophy

What We Offer

A System for Success

Every project is a system. We help you align performance, design, and execution through the following service categories:

Façade & Building Envelope

  • Curtain walls, structural glass
  • Architectural metals & panels
  • Anchoring & waterproofing

Interior Architectural Systems

  • Glass partitions, sliding panels
  • Custom railings, movable walls
  • Acoustic separations

Fabrication & Millwork

  • Wood, metal, stone integrations
  • Finish carpentry, material transitions
  • Bespoke assemblies

Preconstruction & Support

  • Budget estimating, scope dev
  • Shop drawings, submittals, RFIs
  • Value engineering

Project Management

  • On-site coordination
  • Fabricator alignment, logistics
  • Field adjustments

Optional Services
